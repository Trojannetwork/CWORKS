/ about

A small studio, built to last past launch day.

CWORKS started in Kampala with a simple frustration: most businesses either can't afford a proper website, or get one built and then have no one to call when something breaks. We build both the site and the relationship that keeps it working.

We keep the team small on purpose. It means the person who builds your site is the person who answers when you email us six months later.
